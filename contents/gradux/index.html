Gradux | The Photographic Journal

Developing the visual narrative. Gradux operates like a variable aperture for your digital collection, controlling depth of field and exposure. It transforms the static negative into a dynamic print, allowing you to adjust the focal point of your viewing experience. Navigate through grain, contrast, and composition in a space designed to breathe with the imagery it holds.

The Composition of Code

Layout is the new shutter speed. By shifting between the organic grain of Masonry, the contact-sheet discipline of the Grid, the panoramic linearity of Horizontal, and the telephoto isolation of Focus mode, Gradux alters the cognitive exposure of visual data. It empowers the viewer to meter their own pacing and framing.
