The Protocol

Luminora simulates a closed geometric system in constant flux. The goal is simple: outlast the other vectors. As the match progresses, the system injects random anomalies—wormholes, gravity shifts, and velocity surges—to test your adaptability.

Even in death, you remain a threat. The Phoenix Protocol allows ghosts to fire projectiles. If your shot eliminates a survivor within 1 second, you instantly respawn into the match.
