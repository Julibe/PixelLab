Dot Trap

Forget the pencil-and-paper games of your childhood. Dot Trap reimagines the classic "Dots and Boxes" as a high-octane strategic battlefield. Featuring adaptive AI, rhythm-synced audio, and chaos-inducing power-ups, this isn't just about connecting dots—it's about trapping your opponent before the clock runs out.

Beyond the Grid

Originally known as La Pipopipette by French mathematician Édouard Lucas in the 19th century, the game of Dots and Boxes is a study in combinatorial game theory and topology. Dot Trap elevates this mathematical foundation by introducing variable chaos.

By moving beyond the standard square grid to hexagonal, triangular, and "gemstone" lattices, the game challenges your spatial reasoning.
